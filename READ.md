# Content Writing Keyword Research & Ideation Project

This repository contains a structured keyword research and content ideation project tailored for the content writing and blogging domain.

## Brainstorming Methodology
1. **Audience Mapping**: Identified core pain points of aspiring freelance writers, digital marketers, and small business owners.
2. **Seed Selection**: Generated primary core themes around SEO, writing productivity, AI integration, and client acquisition.
3. **Keyword Expansion**: Used keyword research frameworks to find search volumes and difficulty metrics to ensure realistic ranking potential.
4. **Filtering**: Filtered the top 10 most viable ideas based on low-to-moderate Keyword Difficulty (KD) and strong search intent.

## Tool Stack
* **Keyword Research**: Google Keyword Planner / Ubersuggest (Simulated) / coshedule
* **Documentation**: Markdown
* **Version Control**: GitHub
